Challenge Selected

Education Inequality

One of the biggest problems in the world today — and one that doesn't get enough attention. Millions of children are completely locked out of learning, not because they don't want to, but because the resources simply aren't there.

The Problems

Why kids stay locked out

Displacement

  • 250M+ children & youth have no access to formal education.
  • Half of secondary-age kids miss day-to-day language & basic knowledge.
  • Disasters take 12–24 months to rebuild a concrete school.

Funding Issues

  • A permanent low-income school costs $20K–$75K.
  • NGO schools rely on single short-term grants.
  • Tents in underprivileged areas cost upwards of $30K — and aren't safe.

Electricity & Internet

  • 25% of schools in low-income countries have zero electricity.
  • Modern education needs internet — now the default everywhere.
  • Without a grid, children are cut off from the global economy.

Teacher Shortages

  • Training a qualified teacher takes ~5 years.
  • Ratios reach 1:60 — the system is desperately scarce.
  • Certified teachers rarely relocate to high-risk, off-grid areas.
The Solution

A school, delivered.

From delivery to active classrooms in about 5 days. Cargo containers travel globally, snap together like LEGO, and survive anything the environment throws at them.

Infrastructure on Demand

  • Delivery to active classrooms in ~5 days.

  • Expand endlessly — stack and grow bigger over time.

Built for Any Environment

  • Steel-reinforced — ~20 year lifespan.
  • Fully insulated for extreme climate control.
  • Weatherproof: dust storms, rain storms, floods.
